Introduction

Google Drive File Manager ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Google Drive file management application.

Information We Access

When you use Google Drive File Manager, the app may access:

  • Google Drive Files and Folders: Names, sizes, types, and metadata of files and folders in your Google Drive
  • Email Address: Only when you voluntarily submit feedback through the feedback form
  • Google Account Information: Basic profile information required for authentication

How We Use Information

We access your information solely to:

  • Display your files and folders for management purposes
  • Perform requested operations (copy, move, delete, create folders)
  • Send feedback emails when you explicitly request it
  • Authenticate your access to the application

Information Storage and Security

Important: Google Drive File Manager does NOT:

  • Store any of your files or file contents
  • Retain any information about your files after you close the app
  • Share your information with third parties
  • Use your data for advertising or marketing purposes

All operations are performed in real-time through Google's secure APIs, and no data is stored on our servers.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following Google services:

  • Google Drive API: To access and manage your files
  • Gmail API: To send feedback emails (only when you use the feedback feature)
  • Google OAuth 2.0: For secure authentication

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Revoke the app's access to your Google Drive at any time through your Google Account settings
  • Request information about how we handle your data
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

Data Retention

We do not retain any data from your Google Drive. Feedback emails and your email address are retained only for the purpose of responding to your feedback and improving our service.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy.
